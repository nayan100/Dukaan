# Dukaan User Guide

Welcome to Dukaan. This guide will walk you through the primary operational flows of the platform.

## 1. Strategy Hub (Dashboard)
The **Strategy Hub** is your mission control. It provides global visibility into your retail network.

- **Metric Cards:** Monitor real-time Revenue, Margin, and Transactions.
- **Sales Velocity:** View hourly transaction trends to identify peak performance windows.
- **Branch Health:** Compare performance across KTM, Pokhara, and other nodes.
- **Compliance Sentinel:** Watch for automated fraud alerts and IRD sync status.

## 2. Point of Sale (POS)
The **POS HUD** is optimized for high-speed checkout.

- **Adding Items:** Click or tap any product in the grid. Use the search bar for rapid discovery.
- **Voiding Mistakes:** If an error occurs, click **Void** on the item in the sidebar. Note: This is only available for 60 seconds after the item is added.
- **Finalizing Sale:** Press `Enter` or click **Checkout Order**.
- **Split Payments:** Enter the Cash and Digital (QR) amounts received. The system will calculate balance due or change instantly.

## 3. Growth Wizards
Use the **Growth Protocol** to scale your business.

- **Single-to-Chain:** Automates the migration of legacy stock into a multi-branch hierarchy.
- **IRD Onboarding:** Guides you through provisioning security keys for CBMS compliance.
