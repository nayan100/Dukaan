# Track: 002 - Research Roles

## Description
Deep research, grilling, and streamlining of responsibilities for the four core system personas (Admin, Shop Owner, Accountant, Seller) to better align with real-world operations and the Headless ERP approach.

## Status
Completed

## Personas & Responsibilities

### Admin (SaaS Super Admin)
- **SaaS Management:** View total active/inactive shop owners, manage Subscription Plans, and view platform MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue).
- **Access Control:** Activate, suspend, delete, or impersonate tenant accounts for support.
- **Communication Hub:** Dynamic notification system (broadcasts, targeted alerts by topic like 'billing overdue') and a Support Ticketing inbox.

### Shop Owner (Business Tenant)
- **Multi-Branch Operations:** Add/Edit/Delete shops, manage business legal docs (PAN/VAT, certs).
- **Supply Chain & Inventory:** Add product variants, manage Suppliers, issue Purchase Orders, process Goods Receipts, and handle Inter-branch stock transfers.
- **Financial & HR Overview:** 360° dashboard showing consolidated and per-shop P&L, OPEX (rent, utilities), and CAPEX. Manage employee rosters, salaries, and track seller productivity (sales volume per employee).
- **Policy Engine:** Define dynamic store policies (tax brackets, custom service charges, return/refund windows).

### Accountant
- **Ledger & Reconciliations:** Full access to Chart of Accounts, Bank Reconciliations, and manual Journal Entries for OPEX/adjustments.
- **Reporting:** Export Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, and Trial Balance.
- **Compliance:** Generate Nepal-specific tax reports (VAT 13% registers, Purchase/Sales Registers for IRD filing).

### Seller (Cashier/POS Operator)
- **Point of Sale (POS):** Fast barcode scanning, stock lookups, and customer cart management.
- **Checkout & Payments:** Multi-mode payments (Cash, Card, QR), discount applications (based on Owner policies).
- **Shift Operations:** Cash drawer opening/closing, end-of-day shift settlement reports.
- **Customer Service:** Process refunds and returns mapped directly against original digital invoices.
